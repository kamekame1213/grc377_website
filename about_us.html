ABOUT US

At Kazu, our purpose is to provide innovative speaker technologies at an affordable price so people can experience the power of music to the fullest. Kazu was founded in 2000 in Seattle, WA and since then, we have been providing high-quality speakers to our clients all over the world. Today, we have grown to become a big company with more than 10,000 employees with offices across 10 countries.
